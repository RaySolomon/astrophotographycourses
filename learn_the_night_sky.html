Learn the Night Sky : £12 /session

With Mary McIntyre, FRAS

Saturday 8th September, 1st December, 2nd March, 1st June

10am -12:30pm at Tooley's Boatyard, Banbury.


Learn about what is happening in the night sky in the coming 3 months with this family friendly session hosted by Mary McIntyre, a local astronomer and astro-imager. You can read more about Mary here and examples of her images can be found on her website.

The class includes:
  • how to find your way around the night sky
  • how to use the stars as signposts
  • where the planets will be placed
  • conjunctions
  • Moon phase information
  • upcoming meteor showers
  • the mythology of some of this season’s constellations
  • finding some great naked eye, binocular and telescope objects within those constellations.

    • The class is suitable for complete beginners as well budding astronomers who already have some knowledge.
