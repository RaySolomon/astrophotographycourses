Solar Imaging in White Light, Ha and CaK : £35

With Pete Williamson

Saturday 20th July 2019 – 10am - 4.30pm at Tooley's Boatyard, Banbury


This course will cover different equipment used for White Light, Ha and CaK such as cameras & Telescopes.

It will cover Firecapture / Sharpcap etc, for data capture, the use of Autostakkert! for stacking, Registax for sharpening and finally Photoshop and Topaz for finishing.

All will include White Light images, Cak and Ha Chromsphere & Prominences
