Deep Sky Imaging £35

 With Gary Palmer

 Sunday 19th November 10am – 4.00pm at Tooley's Boatyard, Banbury

This course will incorporate: Telescopes types and setups used for imaging, types of cameras Deepsky and DSLR, collimation, focusing, image size, capturing the data, pre- processing of Fits and DSLR files. Final processing, basic use of popular software.
Gary is one of the leading solar imagers in the world. His images have been widely published accross all sorts of media around the world. Prior to that he was mainly a planetary imager and deepsky imager. Gary now concentrates on imaging the Sun at high resolution and creating large mosaics of the Sun. He also does alot of "Solar Outreach" bringing solar viewing to a wide range of people and groups. Gary has a wide ranging knowledge of equipment and how to set it up. He also works with large equipment maunfacturers, and has provided them with images for publication.
