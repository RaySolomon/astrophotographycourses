Learn the Night Sky - Summer: £15

With Mary McIntyre, FRAS

Saturday 9th June – 12am at Tooley's Boatyard, Banbury.


Learn about what is happening in the night in the coming 3 months with this family friendly course hosted by Mary McIntyre.

The class includes:
  • how to find your way around the night sky
  • how to use the stars as signposts
  • where the planets will be placed
  • conjunctions
  • Moon phase information
  • upcoming meteor showers
    • You will learn about the mythology of some of this season’s constellations, then learn how to find some naked eye, binocular and telescope objects within those constellations. Suitable for complete beginners as well budding astronomers who already have some knowledge.
