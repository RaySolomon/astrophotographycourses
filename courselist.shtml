We have teamed up with some leading astro-imaging figures to run a series of courses which will be held at Tooley's Boatyard right by the canal in Banbury.

These astronomy workshops are designed for the experienced as well as the beginner photographer. The courses aim to inspire both practicing and armchair astronomers. We will guide you through what equipment is needed, how to capture and then process the data. For full workshop details please click on the course name below and for more information about the speaker, please click on their name.

The course season runs from September to August.

Date Course With Time Level

Course end-time is approximate as all courses include tea breaks (tea provided) and all-day courses include a break for lunch. Lunch is not provided but there are many options available in the adjacent Castle Quay shopping centre and Banbury town centre a couple of minutes walk away. You're also welcome to bring a packed lunch to eat on premises or in the sunshine by the canal.
