About Paddy
A lifelong fascination with the night sky led me to finally acting on this a little over three years ago. During this time, I have gone from being a distinctly average producer of images to being quite well known and producing some successful and well-liked images.

I have featured in many magazines and won various awards for my imaging. I have also been a speaker at the International Astronomy Show in the UK and NEAIC in the US. My passion is to realize the night sky in all its glory and to share how I do this to encourage others into the hobby.

I am also a co-owner of www.Imagetheuniverse.co.uk a modest business that tries to bring world-class AP data to users and affordable prices.
