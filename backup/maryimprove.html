Improve Your Astrophotography £15

With Mary McIntyre, FRAS

Saturday 28th July – 10 - 12:30 at Tooley's Boatyard, Banbury


This 2 hour workshop follows on from the "Introduction To Astrophotography" talk.

The workshop covers how to stack astronomy images in more detail, including how to shoot calibration frames and why they are important. Mary provides walk-throughs on how to stack Moon, Sun and deep sky images to help you get to grips with the technique.

Lastly Mary covers image processing in more detail, both with free tools like Fastone, Autostakkert and Registax, and how to get started using Adobe Lightroom and finally some Photoshop basics.
