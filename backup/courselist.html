We have teamed up with some leading astro-imaging figures to run a series of courses which will be held at Tooley's Boatyard right by the canal in Banbury.

These astronomy workshops are designed for the experienced as well as the beginner photographer. The courses aim to inspire both practicing and armchair astronomers.

We will guide you through what equipment is needed, how to capture and then process the data.

For full workshop details please click on the course name below.
For more information about the speaker, please click on their name.

The course season runs from September to August and new dates for Autumn 2018 and 2019 will be announced soon.
